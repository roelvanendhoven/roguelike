As the dark night sun consumes the ruins of Gronn, mists shroud the twisted nether, deep below the empire's walls...
A haven for the foul beings that were cast upon the miserable plains of the planet by Moon Lord Phi'll.
For thousands of aeons, peace was kept by the Golden Army of the Lord's Seven Swords.
Until that faithful yet unholy night, when blood gurgled screams filled the foul, rotten, air.

It was then, that all hope vanished. Children fled to their beds, hoping the sheets would provide shelter from that which was to come...
